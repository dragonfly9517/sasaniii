The first app of its kind that brings the common man , the law enforcements and the NGO's on the same platform.


Features :

  1. COMMON MAN / VIEWER :

    • Report any crime you are aware of ( give location ).
    • Get instant access to nearby police departments and related NGO's.
    • Keep a close eye on your neighbour hood.
    • You can view the crime complaints and their crime locations on the map.
    • You can aid your fellow people by signing petitions for their complaints.
    • You can monitor the working of police department and the NGO's.
    • You can like , unlike and comment too.


  2. POLICE DEPARTMENT :

    • View crimes and the number of petitions. Also, they can see the number of crimes which happened in a paticular area.


  3. NGO's :

    • They can find out instantly where help is needed. Even , they can monitor the working of police department too.
